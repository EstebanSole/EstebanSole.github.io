No. 1 - Environmental Protection: Every citizen must plant at least one tree per year and participate in community recycling activities.

No. 2 - Free Education: Basic, secondary, and university education is a guaranteed right for all residents of Goat.

No. 3 - Equality of Rights: All people are equal before the law, regardless of gender, religion, race, or sexual orientation.

No. 4 - Mandatory Coffee Break: At 3 p.m., every person has the right to a coffee or tea break. If someone refuses, it's considered a minor offense!

No. 5 - Goats Have the Right to Participate in Official Meetings: If a goat enters a debate, they must be allowed to express their opinion (even if it's just bleating).

No. 6 - Every Plaza Must Have a Resident Artist: Whether a musician, painter, or storyteller, there must always be someone creating art in public spaces.

No. 7 - Every Year, a "Great Goat President" is Elected by Popular Vote: The goat can come from any background, as long as they show dignity and leadership (or at least don’t run away in the middle of the ceremony).

No. 8 - Protection of Public Green Spaces: Citizens must care for and maintain parks and green areas. Damage to these spaces will result in penalties and the obligation to repair the damages.

No. 9 - Renewable Energy Law: New buildings must be equipped with renewable energy sources, such as solar panels or wind systems, depending on the location. Companies must also adopt clean technologies to reduce their carbon footprint.

No. 10 - Sustainable Transportation Law: The use of public transportation, bicycles, and electric vehicles is encouraged. Fiscal incentives will be implemented for those who use these transportation methods, and excessive use of polluting vehicles will be penalized within the city.
